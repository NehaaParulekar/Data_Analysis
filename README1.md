# 🛒 Walmart Sales Analysis

## 📌 Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze Walmart sales data to identify key trends in revenue, profitability, and customer behavior. The insights derived aim to support data-driven decision-making for improving business performance.

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional sales data including:

* Order Date
* Product Category & Sub-category
* Customer Information
* Sales and Profit
* Region and Location

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* **SQL** → Data querying and analysis
* **Python (pandas, matplotlib)** → Data cleaning and exploratory data analysis
* **Tableau** → Interactive dashboard creation

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

* Sales and profit trend over time
* Category and sub-category performance
* Regional sales distribution
* Top customers and revenue contribution
* Profitability analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Technology category generates the highest revenue but not always the highest profit
* Office Supplies show stable sales but lower profit margins
* Sales show seasonal spikes during specific months
* Certain regions consistently outperform others in both sales and profit
* A small percentage of customers contribute significantly to total revenue

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-margin products instead of only high-revenue items
* Optimize pricing strategies for low-profit categories
* Strengthen customer retention for high-value customers
* Plan inventory based on seasonal demand patterns
* Expand operations in high-performing regions
